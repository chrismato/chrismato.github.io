Biography

My name is Christopher Mato. I'm 13 years old in 8th Grade. I've been playing video games since I was 4 years old, and all my life I always wanted to make my own games. The earliest memory I have from having interest in tech-related stuff was when I played my first video game on my old GameCube. My interest in technology is the whole reason why I'm currently enrolled in a Tech Production class. The boxing glove icon on the top-left of my website represents how I was a strong person growing up. My two main hobbies are sports and video games. The video games I play are games (8-bit NES Games) that look like the kind of games I will make as a video game designer when I grow up. The types of sports I play are Football, Basketball, Baseball, and Soccer. Although I'm not very good at two of these, it's still a ton of fun to play. My dream is to become a video game designer.
